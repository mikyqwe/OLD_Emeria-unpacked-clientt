[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]1. General Informations

On Elendosfiles there is of course also a Costume System.
You can find this in your Inventory.
You get costumes from monsters, events, quests, item shop
What bonuses are there on all Costumes?


[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=280; CENTER_ALIGN]


[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]2. Armor Costume Bonuses

*Max. HP: 2000, 2500, 3000, 4000
*Strong against monsters:  5%, 10%, 15%, 20%, 25%, 30%, 35%, 40%, 45%.
*Chance of critical hit: 5%, 10, 15%, 20%.
*These are the most important bonuses you can get on Armour Costumes.

[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=460; CENTER_ALIGN]


[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]3. Weapon Costume Bonuses

*Vitality: +12
*Intelligence: +12
*Strength: +12
*Dexterity: +12
*Strong against Monsters: 10%, 15%, 20%.
*Strong against Half Human: 10%, 15%, 20%.
*Chance of Critical Hit: 10%
*Chance of Piercing Hit: 10%
*These are the most important bonuses you can get on Weapon Costumes.

[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=710; CENTER_ALIGN]
