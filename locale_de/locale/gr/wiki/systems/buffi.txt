[CENTER_ALIGN]Get your own Buff Shaman and summon her whenever you want.

[COLOR=0xffd700;FONT_SIZE=15]Content List:
*1. Increase the tech levels of the skills
*2. Give your Buffi costumes
*3. Decorate your Buffi with shinings
[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=110; CENTER_ALIGN]


[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]1. Increase the tech levels of the skills

[CENTER_ALIGN] You can simply learn the tech levels of the Buffi skills.
[CENTER_ALIGN] The required items are droppable ingame or in the itemshop.
[CENTER_ALIGN] A buffi compared to a real buff shaman is a bit weaker but easier to handle.



[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]2. Give your Buffi costumes

[CENTER_ALIGN] You can give your Buffi costumes or armors to style her outfit.
[CENTER_ALIGN] The bonuses or remaining time will not be gone after you undress it.



[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]3. Decorate your Buffi with shinings

[CENTER_ALIGN] Customize your Buffi with special shinings. The shining items can be only dressed to Buffi.
[CENTER_ALIGN] * Drop Buffi shinings from monsters
[CENTER_ALIGN] * Purchase Buffi shinings from the itemshop
