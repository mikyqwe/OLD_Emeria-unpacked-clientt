[CENTER_ALIGN]On Elendosfiles there are shinings and wings with different rarities.
[CENTER_ALIGN]The rarer shinings or wings are, the more they are worth.
[CENTER_ALIGN]Let's get right into the details:

[COLOR=0xffd700;FONT_SIZE=15]Content List:
*1. Items
*2. Rarities
[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=105; CENTER_ALIGN]


[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]1. Items

[CENTER_ALIGN] You can drop the containers ingame from high end bosses or via itemshop.
[CENTER_ALIGN] The item drops ingame are low but basically they are the same.
[CENTER_ALIGN] You can trade the containers ingame.



[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]2. Rarities

[CENTER_ALIGN] There are 5 types of rarities:
[CENTER_ALIGN] * Common (50%)
[CENTER_ALIGN] * Uncommon (30%)
[CENTER_ALIGN] * Rare (15%)
[CENTER_ALIGN] * Very Rare (5%)
[CENTER_ALIGN] * Legendary (unknown)
[CENTER_ALIGN] 
[CENTER_ALIGN] The legendary rarity will be currently only used in the wings containers.
